# Product

## Register

product

## Users
College students and classmates playing a quick memory game together between classes, during breaks, or at casual game nights.

## Product Purpose
Deliver a fast, replayable matching challenge that is immediately understandable, satisfying to master, and fun to compare through moves and high scores.

## Brand Personality
Energetic, nostalgic, and social. The voice is confident and playful without feeling childish.

## Anti-references
Avoid toddler-game styling, casino aesthetics, illegible neon overload, generic glass dashboards, and effects that distract from matching cards.

## Design Principles

- Make every action feel immediate and game-like.
- Keep the board as the unquestioned visual and interaction focus.
- Use nostalgia as a coherent system, not a decoration layer.
- Reward progress with concise audio, motion, and status feedback.
- Preserve clarity and control when effects are reduced or muted.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, full keyboard operation, visible focus, readable game status, a sound toggle, and meaningful reduced-motion behavior.
